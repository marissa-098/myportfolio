All About Me



| Welcome to My Corner of the Internet!

Hello there! I’m MARISSA MANRIQUE, and I’m thrilled to share a little bit about myself with you. I believe that every person has a unique story to tell, and I’m excited to share mine.




I was born and raised in Pag-asa, Bansud, Oriental Mindoro, where my passion for technology began. Growing up, I found myself fascinated by computers and their capabilities, even though my family faced financial challenges. Back in high school, we didn’t have computers at home, but that didn't stop my curiosity.

During my studies, I dedicated myself to understanding new technologies and frameworks. My hard work paid off, as I received numerous awards in different subjects, especially in programming and web development. These accolades not only boosted my confidence but also reinforced my commitment to pursue a career in web development. I realized that with determination and effort, I could turn my passion into a profession.

Each award represented not just academic success but also the countless late nights spent studying and practicing coding. I remember the excitement of participating in coding competitions, where I collaborated with peers to create innovative solutions. These experiences shaped my skills and deepened my love for web development, motivating me to keep pushing the boundaries of what I could achieve.

As I continue my journey in this field, I carry with me the lessons learned from my humble beginnings and the drive to make a positive impact through technology. I believe in the power of positivity and the importance of lifelong learning. Every day is an opportunity to grow, connect with others, and make a difference, no matter how small. I’m excited to share my thoughts, experiences, and musings through this blog. Feel free to reach out, I’d love to hear from you!

Thank you for stopping by, and I hope you enjoy the journey ahead!
