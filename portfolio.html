The Student Panel is a user-friendly interface that allows students to access and manage their academic information, including schedules, grades, and financial records. This centralized platform empowers students to stay organized and informed, enhancing their overall educational experience



STUDENT GRADES

The Grades feature allows students to view their academic performance in real-time, providing detailed insights into individual course grades and overall GPA. This tool helps students track their progress and identify areas for improvement, fostering a proactive approach to their education.

The Schedule feature enables students to access their class timetable, including course details, locations, and timings. This tool helps students stay organized and manage their time effectively, ensuring they are prepared for their daily academic activities.



PRINTING FEATURE

The Printing Feature allows students and administrators to easily print schedules, grades, and reports directly from the system. This functionality enhances convenience by providing physical copies for record-keeping or offline access, streamlining administrative tasks.
